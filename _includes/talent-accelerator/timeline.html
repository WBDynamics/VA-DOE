Diffusion Talent Accelerator Phases

Diffusion of Excellence has identified 15 core competencies, which when executed, stimulate a culture of Diffusion, and construct a roadmap for replicating and sustaining innovative efforts within VHA. Diffusion Talent Accelerator Specialists will become proficient in all these competencies over time and will be introduced to them through various exercises, presentations, and real-world applications. Each competency is aligned to a phase of Diffusion Program Development as outlined in the table below.
