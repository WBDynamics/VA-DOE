Our Mission

Diffusion Talent Accelerator (DTA) is a new offering by VHA Innovation Ecosystem (IE) for VISNs and National Program Offices seeking to strengthen their ability to replicate and scale promising practices.

Empower Your Impact:

Collaborate and Innovate Within the VHA Innovation Ecosystem

VHA Innovation Ecosystem is seeking VISNs and National Program Offices that wish to dedicate their FTE to diffusion efforts, to strengthen their operations and expand VHA's human talent pool capable of replicating and scaling Promising Practices.

Diffusion Talent Accelerator Cohort 1 is underway.

Diffusion of Excellence will be recruiting Cohort 2 in January of 2024.
For more information, please email VACODiffusionAccelerator@va.gov.
